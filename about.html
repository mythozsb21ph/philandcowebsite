About PHILANDCO HUMAN RESOURCES CORP.

PHILANDCO began operations in the year 2014. Starting small, PHILANDCO at first limited its deployment to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, supplying household service workers, skilled and semi-skilled workers to its clients. Now, having already honed its procedures and expertise, it is expanding operations to include deployment of workers to most of the major Middle Eastern countries to include Kuwait, United Arab Emirates, Qatar and Bahrain. Not only that, PHILANDCO also has the necessary expertise and resources to deploy skilled Filipino workers to North America, Europe, Oceania, Asia and practically every corner of the globe.

EXPERIENCE

The Filipino workers are widely acknowledged to be one of the most reliable, hardworking and trustworthy in the world. Not to mentioned their proficiency in the English language and with this combination, Overseas Filipino Workers are hard to beat. PHILANDCO can readily provide its clients with such workers.

Through our manpower pooling activities we have access to a database of workers engaged in practically every trade and profession. We can provide clients skilled workers for construction projects, hotel and restaurant services, automotive, electrical and mechanical industries, and the medical care professions such as nurses, medical technicians, nursing aides, and others. PHILANDCO also has the expertise and resources in the deployment of home workers, such as domestic helpers, chauffeurs, nursemaids, and the like.

Our clients can be assured that all Overseas Filipino Workers we deploy are competent, properly certified and trained in their trades or professions.

WHY PHILIPPINES?

Filipinos are proficient in English.

Data shows that over 70% of the populations in the Philippines are proficient in basic English language. In the year 2020, Philippines is at No.2 among all Asian countries who can speak fluent English.

Filipinos are highly skilled and trainable.

Filipinos can learn quickly on their own feet and can easily adopt in any work environment. They have rich experiences that help hone theirs skills in different fields. and a willingness to be trained

Filipinos are hardworking individuals

These people are mostly working to provide for their families and earning for their loved ones is their main motivation to be the best on any given job.
